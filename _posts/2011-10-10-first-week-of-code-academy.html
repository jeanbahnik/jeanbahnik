And what a great first week it was!
Monday we got a surprise visit from @dhh, David Heinemer-Hanson, co-founder of 37Signals and creator of Ruby on Rails. He spent 30 minutes or so with us answering our questions. It was a great way to start.
Tuesday was the first class, where we went trhough some of the basics of Ruby, Rails and HTML. I already knew about that from following Michael Hartl’s classes but it was a great refresher. I also got to met with my mentor for the first time on Tuesday.
Finally we had our first accelerated classes on Tuesday and Saturday, where we discussed everyone’s projects and ideas, as well as ways to improve the program.
Overall: very happy, very satisfied. A Good first week. But it is also showing me how much more I have to learn and do to get BeeBuzz launched. More on this very soon…
