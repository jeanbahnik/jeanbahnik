--- layout: post title: Cleaning up your online presence tags: - programming - career type: post published: false comments: true --- We've been interviewing a lot of people at TC. It makes me wonder how I would do, starting with my online presence. I searched for my name using google chrome's Incognito tab feature A lot of services that I signed up for and am not using I think it's better not to have a service than to look like you're not using it. I started closing down my accounts, using the awesome http://justdelete.me/ It's also about making an effort to stay active on the apps that I choose to keep, like my blog and 500px, G+ After a few weeks, all of the deleted accounts went from 404 error pages to being removed from google's search results.
